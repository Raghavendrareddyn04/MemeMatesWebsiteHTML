Information We Collect
Information We Collect
Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services while respecting your privacy. This includes information you provide, such as contact details (name and email), profile information (username and bio), and any content you post, like memes or comments. We also collect certain information automatically, including usage data (such as app interactions and time spent), device details (like device type and operating system), and log data (including IP address and browser type).

How we use your Information
How we use your Information
How we use your Information

We use your information to provide, improve, and secure our services. This includes operating and maintaining MemeMates so you can access and enjoy the platform smoothly, as well as helping you find and connect with others to build meaningful interactions. We also personalize and enhance your experience to make it more engaging and relevant to you. In addition, we prioritize the safety and security of our community by protecting users and preventing misuse or abuse of the platform.

Information Sharing
Information Sharing
Information Sharing

We are committed to protecting your privacy and limit the sharing of your information. We never sell your personal data to third parties. Information is only shared when it is necessary to operate our services or when required by law. In some cases, we work with trusted service providers who help us deliver and improve MemeMates, and they are only given the information needed to perform their tasks. Additionally, we may share information if it is legally required or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our community.

Your Rights
Your Rights
Your Rights

You have control over your information and how it is used. You can view and modify your personal details at any time, as well as remove any content you have posted on our platform. If you choose, you also have the right to permanently delete your account along with all associated data. In addition, you can request and download a copy of your information in a standard, accessible format.

Data Security
Data Security
Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, ensuring that it remains safe during storage and transfer. Strict access controls are in place to limit who can view your data, and we regularly audit our security practices to maintain the highest standards. In the event of any security incident, we have established response procedures to act quickly and effectively to protect your information and our community.

Children Privacy
Children Privacy
Children Privacy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or industry standards. Whenever updates are made, we will notify users through in-app notifications, emails, or website banners. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information and uphold your privacy rights.
